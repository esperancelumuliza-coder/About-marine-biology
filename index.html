About Marine Biology

What is marine biology?

Marine biology is the study of the sea and the organisms living in it.

Marine biology focuses on:

  1. Marine Ecology
  2. Oceanography
  3. Ichthyology and Mammalogy
  4. Conservation and Environmental Biology
  5. Aquaculture and Fisheries

Why is marine biology important?

Marine biology research contributes to the development of drugs and techonolgy.The research help us to understand how we as humans affect the sea and tbe organisms living in it.The research also helps us to create solutions to decrease marine pollution so that our oceans remain clean for both the marine life and ourselves.
